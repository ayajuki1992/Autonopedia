Robots in our Society

What do Robots do in our society?

Autonomous Robots can be found all over our world. From our medical sectors, to our very own roads. These changes have been happening for quite a while, with one of the first that people may be familiar with is automated cashiers. Self checkout machines have been mainstream since the early 2000's. With new and emerging technologies like Tesla's autopilot for cars, (See below) becoming available to the public, it really does make a person think about what kind of technologies will be a part of life in the near future! Even something as simple as a stairlift is a way to assist our elderlies using autonomous technologies. Another eye-opening view on just how engrained autonomous robots are in our society, think about how you may be able to see at night. That's right! Using smart automated streetlights, we were able to illuminate our world whenever it's convenient. (We at Autonopedia can assure you that there are no little men in the streetlights that wait for night to fall!)
